Vision X Studios — June 2026 Operating Plan
June 3 – June 30, 2026  ·  Charlotte, NC  ·  COO Operating System
ℹ
These are ownership roles, not separate paid positions. Each person is paid for their contracted work. These leadership responsibilities are how we build Vision X together — creating more clients, projects, and revenue for everyone.
Role Ownership — June 2026
Parker
Founder / CEO — Revenue, Sales, Partnerships, Strategy & Socials
Parker owns revenue, strategic direction, key partnerships, and the social media presence. For social: Jay queues all content — Parker approves quickly and posts. Parker should NOT be editing, chasing team members, handling admin, or running operations. Time allocation: 60% sales · 20% partnerships · 10% leadership · 10% strategy/socials.
Jay
Director of Content — Filming, Editing, Scheduling & Content Systems
Jay is the primary content person end to end. He films sessions and spaces, edits all footage, builds the content queue, and schedules everything for Parker to approve and post. Before June 10, Jay must build a content queue deep enough to cover his June 10–15 absence — Parker posts from that queue while Jay is out. Ty and Hakiem can film as backup only when Jay specifically needs coverage.
Ty
Creative Director & Producer — Creative Strategy, Direction & Session Production
Ty thinks like a business strategist with a creative eye. He owns creative direction — how Vision X looks, sounds, and is positioned in the market. He tracks and produces in sessions, brings creative vision to projects, and helps Parker think through how to package and present Vision X's services. He can film when Jay needs backup but that is secondary to his creative and production role.
Hakiem
Community & Artist Development Director — Events, Artists & Membership
Hakiem owns the community side of Vision X. He drives Creator Night June 27, Song Wars artist recruitment, tour hosting for artists, and membership growth conversations. He is the primary connector between Vision X and Charlotte's creative community. He can film as backup when Jay needs coverage but his main job is relationships and events.
Chuk
Studio Operations Coordinator — Facility, Equipment & Presentation
Chuk owns the physical environment. Every studio, lounge, podcast room, warehouse space, photography area, and lobby must be tour-ready at all times. He runs weekly facility checks and resolves any maintenance or equipment issues before they become client-facing problems.
Jonothan
Business Development — Leads, CRM & Tour Scheduling
Jonothan owns the lead pipeline from first contact through to a booked tour or meeting. He works in person and remotely — updating the CRM daily, following up on all inbound leads within 24 hours, and booking tours. He works under Parker's sales direction but operates independently on follow-up and scheduling. He escalates to Parker only when a prospect is ready for a high-level conversation.
Dani
Client Relations & Booking Support — Inquiries, Bookings & Client Experience
Dani owns the client-facing experience from first inquiry through to a confirmed booking. She responds to all inbound inquiries, confirms bookings, coordinates scheduling between clients and the studio, and makes sure clients feel taken care of at every step. She is the front line between Vision X and paying clients.
